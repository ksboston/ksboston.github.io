About Kaitlin

Kaitlin is a certified elementary (1-6) and special (K-12, mild/moderate cross-categorical) education teacher in Missouri. She's also a literacy specialist for grades K-12. Kaitlin is originally from western New York, where she attended Niagara University (Vincentian affiliation) for elementary and special education, with a focus on literacy education. Immediately after her undergraduate education, Kaitlin started graduate school at the University at Buffalo (SUNY), where she earned her masters of education in literacy -- finishing in 2013. Throughout her education, Kaitlin worked and volunteered in many schools across western New York. Immediately after graduate school, Kaitlin moved to Saint Louis, Missorui, where she started working in the Missouri Schools for the Severely Disabled. Starting in December 2014, Kaitlin began her tenure in the Special School District of Saint Louis County, where she has been assigned to Jennings, MO's Fairview Primary School.
